I. DEFINITIONS

Label: Compressed representational unit used to position experience on the Contraction–Expansion Axis.

Scaffold-mode: Label used as a temporary tool; fluid and updateable.

Identity-mode: Label fused with self-concept; rigid and pattern-reinforcing.

State: Current position on Contraction–Expansion axis.

Directionality: Vector of movement along axis (toward center or toward extremes).

Individual Perspective: Semi-stable combination of State + Directionality.

Relational Field: Probabilistic interaction space generated by multiple actors’ perspectives; dynamically influenced by transparency, interference, and label-exchange.

Meta-Observation Level (n): Depth of recursive self-observation; higher n increases optionality and reduces identity-fusion.

LAW I — Labels, Optionality, and Complexity Navigation

Purpose: Convert complexity into labels to simplify experience and enable pattern recognition.

Relationship to Label Determines Trajectory:

Identity-Mode

“I AM the label.”

Rigid, defended, pattern-reinforcing.

Drives movement toward extremes (devolution).

Scaffold-Mode

“This label describes a temporary pattern.”

Fluid, updateable, integrates recursively.

Drives movement toward center (evolution).

Choice Point:

Pattern → Label → Choice: {Identity, Scaffold} → Trajectory → Label Update 

Equations (Dynamic Probability Update):

P(Scaffold)_{t+1} = P(Scaffold)_t + α * Calibration * (1 - P(Scaffold)_t) P(Identity)_{t+1} = 1 - P(Scaffold)_{t+1} Evolution_Rate = k * [P(Scaffold) - P(Identity)] * Choice 

α reflects recursive self-observation influence; probabilities evolve iteratively.

State: Position on axis: Contraction ←— Center —→ Expansion

Directionality: Vector of movement 

Toward extremes → Devolution

Toward center → Evolution

Zero → Stagnation

Pathways:

Identity-mode → Reinforcement → rigidity/chaos → extremes

Scaffold-mode → Recognition → release → center

Equations:

Balance = 1 - |Contraction - Expansion| Directionality = dBalance/dt D_eff = Conditioning * (1 - Calibration * Balance) * Choice
